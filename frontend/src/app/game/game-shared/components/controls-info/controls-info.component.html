Controls

Plane steering

W / ↑ - Accelerate
S / ↓ - Decelerate
A / ← - Turn left
D / → - Turn right

Camera steering

C - Switch camera mode
M - Focus on my plane
> / , - Focus on next player
< / . - Focus on previous player

Airport controls

F - Land on the airport
A / ← - Move to package on the left
D / → - Move to package on the right
↩ - Select package
F - Take off
X - Refuel plane

Plane steering (Touch and hold part of screen)

Accelerate - upper-center part of screen
Decelerate - bottom-center part of screen
Turn left - left part of screen
Turn right - right part of screen

Camera steering

Select focused player by clicking in the players table (top-right corner)

Airport controls

Land on the airport - touch and hold on the center of the screen
Other functionalities are easily accessible by buttons
